1. Introduction

Welcome to MYSpeedZ! This Privacy Policy explains how this app ("we," "us," or "our"), as the Data Controller, collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application, MYSpeedZ (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you. Under regulations like the CCPA, this data falls into specific categories.

a. Information You Provide Directly:

  • Identifiers: Such as your name and email address.
  • Personal Information categories listed in the California Customer Records statute: Such as your name, address, telephone number, driver's license number, and insurance policy number, should you choose to provide them.
  • Account Credentials: Your password for the Service is managed by Firebase Authentication. We do not have direct access to your plain-text password.

b. Information Collected Automatically When You Use the Service:

  • Geolocation Data: Precise GPS location, speed, and heading during active trips.
  • Sensory Data: With your permission, we access data from your device's motion and fitness sensors to automatically detect driving activity.
  • Commercial Information: Records of services purchased, such as your MYSpeedZ Pro subscription history, managed via Apple's In-App Purchase system.
  • Inferences: We generate inferences from the data above, such as your calculated driving score and driving style (e.g., "smooth," "aggressive").

3. How and Why We Use Your Information (Lawful Basis)

Under GDPR, we must have a "lawful basis" for processing your data. We use your information for the following purposes based on the corresponding legal grounds:

To Provide Our Service (Based on Contractual Necessity):

We process your data to fulfill our contract with you, which is our Terms of Use. This includes:

  • Operating and maintaining the Service.
  • Logging your trips, calculating your driving score, and identifying driving events.
  • Displaying your trip history and statistics to you.
  • Enabling social features with friends you have approved.

For Business Improvement (Based on our Legitimate Interests):

We have a legitimate interest in improving our Service and keeping it secure. This includes:

  • Analyzing usage to understand how our app is used and how to improve it.
  • Ensuring the security of our app and backend services.

To Comply with Legal Obligations:

We may process your data where it is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation to which we are subject.

With Your Explicit Consent (Based on Consent):

We will ask for your consent before:

  • Sending you push notifications.
  • Accessing your device's location and motion activity data (the OS will prompt you for this).

4. How We Share Your Information

  • With Your Friends: Your public profile information and trip data are shared with users you have explicitly approved as friends. You can manage these permissions in the app's settings.
  • With Service Providers: We use third-party companies to help us operate our Service. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data. They include:
    • Google (Firebase): For user authentication, database hosting, and push notifications.
    • Google Maps Platform, TomTom, OpenStreetMap: For maps and road data (speed limits, etc.).
    • Apple: For processing In-App Purchases for subscriptions.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Location Data and Driving Information

  • Trip Recording: The Service records your location, speed, and driving behavior during trips. This data is used to calculate driving scores and provide trip analytics.
  • Background Location: With your permission, the Service may access location data in the background to detect when you start and stop driving.
  • Data Accuracy: Location data accuracy depends on your device's GPS capabilities and environmental factors.
  • Data Sharing: Your trip data is only shared with approved friends and is not sold to third parties.

7. Siri and Voice Commands

The Service integrates with Siri for voice-activated trip control:

  • Voice commands are processed by Apple's Siri service
  • Voice data is handled according to Apple's privacy policy
  • You can disable Siri integration in your device settings
  • Voice commands are only available to Pro subscribers

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Service may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze app usage and performance
  • Provide personalized content and features

You can control cookie settings through your device's privacy settings.

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  • Provide details about what data was affected
  • Outline steps we're taking to address the breach
  • Recommend actions you should take to protect yourself

10. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. We may also retain information for a longer period to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

11. Your Data Protection Rights

We are committed to ensuring you have control over your personal data. Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

a. Your Rights under GDPR (for users in the European Economic Area & UK):

  • Right of Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Object to Processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

b. Your Rights under CCPA (for California residents):

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing: We do not "sell" your personal information in the traditional sense of the word. However, the CCPA has a broad definition. We do not share your data with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising. You can be assured we will not sell or share your data.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

c. How to Exercise Your Rights:

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our dedicated email: myspeedzofficial@gmail.com. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.
