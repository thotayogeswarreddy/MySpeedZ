Getting Started

Your Driving Profile

Everything you need to know to start using Drivile effectively

Download & Install

Download Drivile from the App Store and install it on your iPhone. The app is optimized for iOS devices and requires iOS 14.0 or later.

Location Permissions

Grant location access when prompted to enable trip tracking and navigation features. Choose "Allow While Using App" for the best experience.

Notifications

Enable notifications to receive alerts for speed violations, trip summaries, and important driving insights. You can customize these in Settings.

Initial Setup

Complete your profile setup by adding your driving preferences, speed buffer settings, and connecting with friends for the full experience.

Pro Tip: For the best experience, keep location services enabled and allow background app refresh. This ensures accurate trip tracking and real-time features.

Friends System Guide

Your Driving Profile

Learn how the friends system works with real examples of two friends Driv and Ile

Adding Friends

Example: Driv wants to add Ile as a friend. Driv sends a friend request to Ile's email. Ile receives a notification and can accept or decline. Once accepted, they become friends and can see each other's trips.

Trip Notifications

Example: When Driv completes a trip, Ile (if subscribed) receives a notification about Driv's trip completion, score, and any violations. This helps friends stay connected and motivated.

Hiding Trips from Friends

Example: If Driv wants to hide his trips from Ile, he goes to Ile's profile and toggles "Hide my trips from this friend." Now Ile won't see Driv's trips in her feed, but Driv can still see Ile's trips.

Hiding Friend's Trips

Example: If Ile wants to hide Driv's trips from her feed, she goes to Driv's profile and toggles "Hide this friend's trips from my feed." Now Ile won't see Driv's trips, but Driv can still see Ile's trips.

Leaderboard & Competition

Example: Driv and Ile can compete on the leaderboard based on their driving scores. They can see each other's weekly/monthly performance and challenge each other to improve their scores.

Subscription Benefits

Example: If Driv has a Pro subscription, he gets access to advanced analytics, unlimited friend connections, and detailed driving insights that he can share with Ile.

Privacy & Security Features

Complete control over your data and friend interactions

Unilateral Privacy Control

Example: When Driv hides his trips from Ile, it only affects what Ile can see. Ile's privacy settings remain unchanged, and she can still control her own trip visibility independently.

Real-time Privacy Updates

Example: When Driv changes his privacy settings, Ile's feed updates immediately. No need to restart the app or wait for changes to take effect.

Complete Unfriend Cleanup

Example: When Driv unfriends Ile, all connections are severed: no more trip notifications, no access to each other's trips, and all privacy settings are reset automatically.

Location Access & Privacy

Your Driving Profile

Understand exactly when and why Drivile accesses your location data

Foreground Usage

When App Is Open: Drivile accesses your location to display your current position, show speed limits, render maps, and enable navigation features. This is standard for any location-based app.

Background Usage

When App Is Closed: Background location access only occurs when Auto Trip Tracking is enabled AND motion sensors detect you're driving. If you're stationary, background tracking stops automatically.

Motion Sensors

Smart Detection: Motion activity (automotive vs. stationary) helps detect when to start/stop auto trips without constant location polling. This saves battery and respects your privacy.

User Controls

Full Control: You can enable/disable Auto Trip Tracking, Sound Alerts, Friend Alerts, and other features. Changes take effect in real time. You can also change permissions in iOS Settings.

Privacy First: Drivile only tracks location when necessary for core functionality. When you're not driving or have disabled auto-trip tracking, location access is minimized.

Location Tracking Scenarios

Summary: The app tracks location based on a combination of app state, auto-trip setting, motion activity, and trip status. The following table provides a concise overview.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Driving Profile

Find quick answers to the most common questions about Drivile

How does auto trip tracking work?

Drivile uses your iPhone's motion sensors and location data to automatically detect when you start driving. When the app senses automotive motion with high confidence, it begins tracking your trip. The trip ends when you stop moving or when the motion sensor detects you've arrived at your destination.

Is my location data private and secure?

Yes, absolutely. All your location data is encrypted and stored securely on your device and our servers. We never share your personal information with third parties. You can control your privacy settings and delete your data at any time through the app settings.

How accurate is the speed limit detection?

Our speed limit detection uses multiple data sources including GPS, road databases, and real-time traffic information to provide highly accurate speed limits. The accuracy rate is over 95% in most areas, though it may vary in rural or newly constructed roads.

Can I use Drivile without an internet connection?

Yes, Drivile works offline for basic trip tracking and speed monitoring. However, features like route optimization, real-time traffic data, and friend network features require an internet connection. Your data will sync when you're back online.

How do I connect with friends on Drivile?

You can connect with friends by sending them an invitation through the app. Go to the Friends section, tap "Add Friend," and enter their email or username. Once they accept, you can share driving experiences, compare scores, and motivate each other to drive safely.

What's the difference between free and Pro features?

The free version includes basic trip tracking and speed monitoring. Pro features include advanced analytics, friend network, route optimization, detailed driving insights, and priority support. You can try Pro features with a 7-day free trial.

How do Siri shortcuts work with Drivile?

Drivile integrates with Siri to provide hands-free control. You can say "Hey Siri, start my trip" to begin tracking, "Hey Siri, end my trip" to stop tracking, and "Hey Siri, what's my driving score?" to get your performance metrics. Set up custom shortcuts in Settings > Siri & Search > Shortcuts.

How does the friends privacy system work?

Privacy controls are unilateral - when you hide your trips from a friend, it only affects what they can see, not their settings. You can hide your trips from specific friends or hide their trips from your feed. When you unfriend someone, all connections are automatically severed and notifications stop.

What happens when I unfriend someone?

When you unfriend someone, all connections are completely severed: they can no longer see your trips, you can't see their trips, all trip notifications stop, and both users are removed from each other's followers lists. This ensures complete privacy and data separation.

How do trip notifications work between friends?

When you complete a trip, friends who have subscribed to your trip notifications will receive alerts about your trip completion, driving score, and any violations. You can control who receives these notifications by managing your followers list in your profile settings.

Can I use Drivile completely hands-free?

Yes! With Siri integration, you can start and end trips, check your driving score, and get voice updates without touching your phone. This is especially useful for safety while driving. Just make sure to set up your Siri shortcuts first in the app settings.

When does Drivile access my location in the background?

Drivile only accesses your location in the background when Auto Trip Tracking is enabled AND motion sensors detect you're driving. If you're stationary, background tracking stops automatically. When the app is in the foreground, location access is always active for map display and speed monitoring.

How can I control when Drivile tracks my location?

You have full control through the app settings. You can enable/disable Auto Trip Tracking, Sound Alerts, and other features. Changes take effect immediately. You can also change location permissions in iOS Settings (Never / Ask Next Time / While Using / Always) at any time.
