‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does automatic trip detection work?
MYSpeedZ uses advanced motion detection and location tracking to automatically detect when you start driving. The app monitors your movement patterns and begins recording trips when it detects driving behavior.
Why isn't my driving score improving?
Your driving score is calculated based on speed violations, stop sign compliance, and driving behavior. Focus on staying within speed limits, coming to complete stops at stop signs, and driving smoothly to improve your score.
How accurate is the speed limit detection?
MYSpeedZ uses real-time mapping data to provide accurate speed limit information. The system updates as you drive and provides the most current speed limits for your location.
Can I use MYSpeedZ without location services?
Location services are required for trip tracking, speed limit detection, and navigation features. Without location access, most core features will not function properly.
How do I set up Siri voice commands?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Siri Voice Commands ‚Üí Add Start Trip Shortcut and Add Stop Trip Shortcut. Follow the prompts to set up your voice commands. This feature is available for Pro subscribers.
What's the difference between Free and Pro versions?
Free version includes basic trip logging and speed detection. Pro version adds Siri voice commands, advanced analytics, unlimited trip history, and priority support.
