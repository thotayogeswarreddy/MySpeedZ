Getting Started

1. Installation & Setup

  • Download Drivile from the App Store
  • Open the app and create your account
  • Verify your email address
  • Grant necessary permissions for location and motion detection

2. First Trip

  • Ensure location services are enabled
  • Start a manual trip or enable auto-trip tracking

3. Understanding Your Dashboard

  • View your current driving score

Common Questions

Auto-Trip Tracking

Q: How does auto-trip tracking work?
A: Drivile uses your device's motion sensors and location services to automatically detect when you start and stop driving. The app intelligently distinguishes between walking, cycling, and driving to provide accurate trip detection.

Privacy & Data

Q: Is my driving data private?
A: Absolutely! You have full control over your privacy settings. You can choose what to share with friends, control location permissions, and delete your data at any time. We never sell your personal information.

Driving Score

Q: How is my driving score calculated?
A: Your driving score is based on multiple factors including speed limit compliance, stop sign adherence, driving smoothness, and trip duration. The algorithm provides a comprehensive assessment of your driving behavior.

Battery & Performance

Q: Does Drivile drain my battery?
A: Drivile is optimized for minimal battery impact. We use efficient location tracking and intelligent motion detection to balance accuracy with battery life. Most users report minimal additional battery drain.

Troubleshooting

Location Issues

  • Ensure location services are enabled in device settings
  • Grant "Always" permission for background tracking
  • Check that the app has location access
  • Restart the app if issues persist

Auto-Tracking Not Working

  • Verify motion & fitness permissions are granted
  • Check that auto-tracking is enabled in app settings
  • Ensure the app is not force-closed
  • Try toggling auto-tracking off and on

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Update to the latest version of Drivile
  • Restart your device
  • Clear app cache and data
  • Reinstall the app if problems continue
