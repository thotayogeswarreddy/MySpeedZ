Getting Started

Your Driving Profile

Everything you need to know to start using Drivile effectively

Download & Install

Download Drivile from the App Store and install it on your iPhone. The app is optimized for iOS devices and requires iOS 14.0 or later.

Location Permissions

Grant location access when prompted to enable trip tracking and navigation features. Choose "Allow While Using App" for the best experience.

Notifications

Enable notifications to receive alerts for speed violations, trip summaries, and important driving insights. You can customize these in Settings.

Initial Setup

Complete your profile setup by adding your driving preferences, speed buffer settings, and connecting with friends for the full experience.

Pro Tip: For the best experience, keep location services enabled and allow background app refresh. This ensures accurate trip tracking and real-time features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Driving Profile

Find quick answers to the most common questions about Drivile

How does auto trip tracking work?

Drivile uses your iPhone's motion sensors and location data to automatically detect when you start driving. When the app senses automotive motion with high confidence, it begins tracking your trip. The trip ends when you stop moving or when the motion sensor detects you've arrived at your destination.

Is my location data private and secure?

Yes, absolutely. All your location data is encrypted and stored securely on your device and our servers. We never share your personal information with third parties. You can control your privacy settings and delete your data at any time through the app settings.

How accurate is the speed limit detection?

Our speed limit detection uses multiple data sources including GPS, road databases, and real-time traffic information to provide highly accurate speed limits. The accuracy rate is over 95% in most areas, though it may vary in rural or newly constructed roads.

Can I use Drivile without an internet connection?

Yes, Drivile works offline for basic trip tracking and speed monitoring. However, features like route optimization, real-time traffic data, and friend network features require an internet connection. Your data will sync when you're back online.

How do I connect with friends on Drivile?

You can connect with friends by sending them an invitation through the app. Go to the Friends section, tap "Add Friend," and enter their email or username. Once they accept, you can share driving experiences, compare scores, and motivate each other to drive safely.

What's the difference between free and Pro features?

The free version includes basic trip tracking and speed monitoring. Pro features include advanced analytics, friend network, route optimization, detailed driving insights, and priority support. You can try Pro features with a 7-day free trial.
