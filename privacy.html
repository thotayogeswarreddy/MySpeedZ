Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At Drivile, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Your Privacy Matters: We believe in transparency and giving you full control over your data. This policy explains exactly what information we collect, why we need it, and how you can control it.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account and use Drivile, we collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, first name, last name, and profile picture
  • Profile Details: Date of birth, gender, address, phone number, and vehicle information
  • Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, and privacy choices

1.2 Location and Motion Data

To provide our core driving analytics services, we collect:

  • GPS Coordinates: Real-time location data during trips for route tracking and safety monitoring
  • Motion Data: Device movement patterns to detect driving behavior and trip start/stop
  • Trip Information: Start/end locations, route details, and driving duration

1.3 Driving Behavior Data

We collect information about your driving patterns to provide safety insights:

  • Speed Data: Vehicle speed and speed limit compliance
  • Safety Metrics: Stop sign compliance, harsh braking, and acceleration patterns
  • Performance Analytics: Driving scores, violation counts, and improvement trends

1.4 Usage and Analytics Data

To improve our services, we collect:

  • App Usage: Features used, time spent in app, and interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: App crashes, error logs, and technical diagnostics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version

1.5 Advanced Analytics and Tracking

To provide comprehensive driving insights, we also collect:

  • Firebase Analytics: App usage patterns, feature adoption, and performance metrics
  • Push Notifications: Device tokens for friend violation alerts and trip notifications
  • Siri Integration: Voice command processing and natural language understanding
  • Background Processing: Continuous location and motion monitoring for auto-trip detection
  • Audio Data: Sound alert functionality and audio session management
  • Social Interactions: Friend connections, privacy preferences, and sharing settings

1.6 Data Export and Sharing

We collect data related to:

  • PDF Generation: Trip data compilation for report creation and sharing
  • Social Features: Friend system data, leaderboards, and privacy controls
  • Third-Party Integrations: Google Maps routing, TomTom speed limits, and Firebase services

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

We use your information to provide and improve our services:

  • Track and analyze your driving trips and behavior
  • Calculate driving scores and safety metrics
  • Provide real-time speed limit and stop sign monitoring
  • Generate detailed trip reports and analytics
  • Enable social features and friend connections

2.2 Service Improvement

We use aggregated, anonymized data to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Optimize driving algorithms and safety detection
  • Conduct research and analysis

2.3 Communication

We may use your contact information to:

  • Send important app updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send security alerts and account notifications
  • Share product updates and new features (with your consent)

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data: Drivile will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.1 With Your Consent

We may share your information in these specific circumstances:

  • Social Features: With friends you choose to connect with, based on your privacy settings
  • Data Export: When you request to download or export your trip data
  • Third-Party Integrations: With services you explicitly authorize (e.g., Google Maps)

3.2 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the App:

  • Cloud Services: Firebase for data storage and authentication
  • Maps and Navigation: Google Maps and TomTom for location services
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics for app performance insights
  • Payment Processing: App Store for subscription management

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect rights and safety:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate potential violations of our Terms of Use
  • Prevent fraud or security threats

4. Data Security and Protection

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization protocols
  • Secure Infrastructure: Cloud-based security with regular security audits
  • Data Minimization: We only collect data necessary for app functionality

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Account Deletion: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Backup Data: Secure backups are maintained for disaster recovery

5. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • View Your Data: Access all personal information we have about you
  • Update Information: Correct or modify your profile and preferences
  • Delete Data: Remove specific data or your entire account
  • Data Portability: Download your data in a standard format

5.2 Privacy Settings

You can control your privacy through app settings:

  • Location Permissions: Choose when and how location data is collected
  • Social Sharing: Control what information is visible to friends
  • Notification Preferences: Manage what notifications you receive
  • Data Collection: Opt out of certain analytics and tracking

5.3 Communication Preferences

You can control how we communicate with you:

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Choose notification frequency and types
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities
  • Request changes to your communication preferences

6. Third-Party Services and Integrations

6.1 Third-Party Services

Drivile integrates with several third-party services to enhance functionality:

  • Google Maps: For navigation, routing, and location services
  • TomTom: For speed limit detection and traffic information
  • Firebase: For data storage, authentication, and analytics
  • App Store: For app distribution and subscription management

6.2 Third-Party Privacy Policies

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and data practices. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they handle your information.

7. Children's Privacy

Drivile is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses for data protection
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Other appropriate safeguards as required by law

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • We will notify you through the app or email for significant changes
  • Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance
  • You can review the current policy at any time in the app

10. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  • Provide details about what information was affected
  • Explain the steps we're taking to address the breach
  • Offer guidance on steps you can take to protect yourself

12. Your Rights Under Data Protection Laws

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under data protection laws such as GDPR, CCPA, or others:

  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

13. Acknowledgment and Consent

By using Drivile, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. You consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this policy.

Your Privacy is Our Priority: We are committed to protecting your data and providing you with full control over your privacy. If you have any concerns or questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
